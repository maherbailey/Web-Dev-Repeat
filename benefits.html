The Benefits of Education

Education provides knowledge, skills, and opportunities. It empowers people to improve their lives and make positive changes in society. Education can shape lives. People who recive a better education than others tend to do more well in life so I think we should be striving to make a good education more acceseable to everyone and there should be a higher standard when it comes to early life education such as Primary and Secondary School. I personally also think that here in Ireland we should revise and change our points system to get into college as it is unfair in the sense that a person could have a really bad day during one of their Leaving Cert exams and it could cause them to not do as well as they normally would. I think that exams should be done through out the year and a person should be evaluated on that as the Leaving cert in short relies on how good you will do in a one week timeframe which is unfair as I mentioned above a person could go be throughing a horrible time in their life and that could affect their preformance. Main Advantages

  • Better job opportunities and financial security.
  • Improved health and decision-making skills.
  • Social awareness and cultural understanding.
  • Increases chances of getting a better jon with higher pay.
  • Education can increase self esteem by letting you express your ideas more clearly.
  • Education can increase awareness of issues in the world.
  • Helps to learn reading, writing, to improve maths ability and to solve problems.
